## Provider Platform Overview

### Introduction for Healthcare Providers

My Family Clinic provides a comprehensive platform for healthcare providers to connect with patients, manage appointments, participate in Singapore's Healthier SG program, and streamline clinic operations. Our platform is designed specifically for Singapore's healthcare ecosystem and integrates seamlessly with government systems.

## Clinic Registration and Setup

### Initial Clinic Registration

**Registration Requirements:**
- Valid business registration in Singapore
- Medical clinic license from MOH
- Singapore Medical Council registration for doctors
- Appropriate clinic accreditations (CHAS, CDMP, MediSave, etc.)

**Registration Process:**
1. **Account Creation**
   - Visit provider registration page
   - Create clinic administrator account
   - Verify email and phone number
   - Set up secure password with two-factor authentication

2. **Clinic Information Submission**
   - Complete clinic profile form
   - Upload required documentation
   - Provide business registration details
   - Submit medical license information

3. **Verification Process**
   - Document verification (5-7 business days)
   - Site inspection if required
   - Professional reference checks
   - Final approval and account activation

### Required Documentation

**Business Documentation:**
- Business registration certificate
- Goods and Services Tax (GST) registration
- Professional indemnity insurance
- Bank account details for payments
