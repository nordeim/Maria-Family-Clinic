## Getting Started

## Account Setup & Management

### Creating Your Account

#### Step 1: Registration
1. Visit our website or download the mobile app
2. Click "Sign Up" or "Create Account"
3. Choose your preferred registration method:
   - **Email Registration**: Create account with email and password
   - **SingPass Login**: Use your Singapore Digital Identity (recommended)
   - **Mobile Number**: Register with Singapore phone number

#### Step 2: Basic Information
Provide the following information:
- **Full Name** (as per NRIC/FIN)
- **NRIC/FIN Number** (for verification)
- **Date of Birth**
- **Contact Information**:
  - Mobile phone number
  - Email address
  - Residential address
- **Preferred Language**: Choose from English, Mandarin, Malay, Tamil

#### Step 3: Verification
- **SMS Verification**: Enter the 6-digit code sent to your mobile
- **Email Verification**: Check your email and click the verification link
- **SingPass Verification**: Complete identity verification through SingPass

#### Step 4: Profile Setup
Complete your profile with:
- **Emergency Contact**: Name and phone number
- **Medical Information**: Allergies, chronic conditions, current medications
- **Insurance Details**: MediSave, MediShield Life, private insurance
- **Preferred Clinics**: Save favorite clinics for quick access
- **Notification Preferences**: Choose how you want to receive updates

### Managing Your Profile

#### Updating Personal Information
1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to "Profile" or "Account Settings"
3. Click "Edit Profile"
4. Update required information
5. Save changes

**Important**: Keep your contact information up to date to receive important healthcare notifications.

#### Privacy Settings
Control how your information is shared:

**Data Sharing Options:**
- **With Healthcare Providers**: Share relevant health information with your chosen doctors
- **Healthier SG Program**: Allow data sharing with MOH for program benefits
- **Research Participation**: Optionally contribute anonymized data for healthcare research
- **Marketing Communications**: Receive updates about health programs and services

**How to Update Privacy Settings:**
1. Go to "Privacy & Security" in your account settings
2. Toggle preferences for each data sharing option
3. Save your preferences

#### Account Security

**Password Management:**
- Use a strong password with at least 8 characters
- Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- Change your password every 6 months
- Never share your password with others

**Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):**
1. Enable 2FA in account settings
2. Choose authentication method:
   - SMS verification (recommended)
   - Authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy)
   - Email verification
3. Complete setup by verifying your chosen method

**Security Best Practices:**
- Log out after each session, especially on shared devices
- Don't save passwords on public computers
- Regularly review your account activity
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## Healthier SG Program

### About Healthier SG

**What is Healthier SG?**
Healthier SG is Singapore's national preventive healthcare program launched by the Ministry of Health (MOH) in July 2023. The program aims to help all Singapore residents aged 40 and above take proactive steps to manage their health through personalized health plans with family doctors.

**Key Objectives:**
- One resident, one family doctor
- Personalized health plans for preventive care
- Early detection through regular screenings
- Subsidized healthcare services
- Chronic disease management and prevention

### Eligibility Requirements

**Age Requirement:**
- Minimum age: 40 years old
- No upper age limit
- Both Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents eligible

**Residency Status:**
- Singapore Citizens (SC)
- Singapore Permanent Residents (PR)
- Must be physically present in Singapore

**Current Enrollment Status (2025):**
- Open enrollment for all eligible residents aged 40+
- Priority given to residents aged 60+ and chronic disease patients
- Continuous enrollment available throughout the year

### Enrollment Process

#### Step 1: Check Eligibility
1. **Age Verification**: Ensure you are 40 years or older
2. **Residency Status**: Confirm SC or PR status
3. **Health Status Check**: Determine if you have priority conditions:
   - Diabetes
   - Hypertension (high blood pressure)
   - High cholesterol
   - Cardiovascular disease
   - Mental health conditions

#### Step 2: Choose Your Doctor and Clinic
**Finding Participating Doctors:**
1. Use our "Find Healthier SG Doctors" feature
2. Filter for Healthier SG participating clinics
3. Review doctor profiles and specializations
4. Consider location, language, and availability

**Clinic Selection Criteria:**
- **Location**: Convenient access from your home or work
- **Languages**: Doctors who speak your preferred language
- **Services**: Availability of required health services
- **Operating Hours**: Compatible with your schedule
- **Doctor-Patient Compatibility**: Choose a doctor you're comfortable with

#### Step 3: Enroll Through HealthHub
1. **Access HealthHub**: Download the HealthHub mobile app or visit healthhub.sg
2. **SingPass Login**: Use your SingPass to access your account
3. **Select Program**: Navigate to Healthier SG enrollment
4. **Choose Doctor**: Select your preferred participating doctor
5. **Confirm Enrollment**: Review and submit your enrollment
6. **Receive Confirmation**: Get SMS and email confirmation

#### Step 4: Schedule Your First Consultation
**Timeline Requirement:**
- Must schedule first consultation within 3 months of enrollment
- Book appointment through My Family Clinic or directly with clinic
- Bring NRIC/FIN and any relevant medical records

**First Consultation Process:**
1. **Health Assessment**: Comprehensive health evaluation
2. **Medical History Review**: Discussion of past health conditions
3. **Risk Factor Identification**: Assessment of health risks
4. **Health Plan Development**: Personalized prevention plan
5. **Goal Setting**: Establish health improvement targets

**After First Consultation:**
- Receive $20 worth of Healthpoints (for Singapore Citizens)
- Get your personalized Health Plan document
- Schedule follow-up appointments if needed
- Begin preventive care activities

A: Most clinics allow booking up to 4 weeks in advance. Some offer same-day or urgent care appointments.

**Q: What if I need to cancel my appointment?**
A: Cancel online up to 2 hours before your appointment to avoid penalties. Call the clinic directly for last-minute cancellations.

**Q: Can I book appointments for family members?**
A: Yes, you can book for family members if they are authorized on your account or with their consent.

**Q: What should I do if I'm running late?**
A: Call the clinic as soon as possible. Most clinics can accommodate 10-15 minutes of lateness; beyond that may require rescheduling.
