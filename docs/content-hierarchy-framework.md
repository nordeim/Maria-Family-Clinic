# Healthier SG Content Hierarchy & Framework Design
## Sub-Phase 8.1: Multilingual Content Strategy & Structure

**Date:** November 4, 2025  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Author:** Content Strategy Team  
**Status:** Implementation Ready  

---

## Executive Summary

This document defines the comprehensive content hierarchy and multilingual framework for Healthier SG program integration within the My Family Clinic platform. It provides detailed specifications for content organization, localization workflows, governance structures, and user experience design across Singapore's four official languages.

---

## 1. Content Architecture Overview

### 1.1 Content Hierarchy Structure

The Healthier SG content architecture follows a hierarchical tree structure that mirrors user mental models and supports efficient navigation while maintaining government compliance standards.

```
Healthier SG Content Tree

│   ├── What is Healthier SG
│   ├── Program Benefits
│   ├── Eligibility Criteria
│   └── Timeline & Milestones
├── Enrollment Journey
│   ├── Pre-Enrollment Information
│   ├── Step-by-Step Enrollment Guide
│   ├── Caregiver Assistance Options
│   └── Post-Enrollment Process
├── Health Management
│   ├── Health Plan Development
│   ├── Screening Programs
│   ├── Vaccination Schedule
│   ├── Chronic Disease Management
│   └── Lifestyle & Wellness
├── Provider Network
│   ├── Clinic Search & Selection
│   ├── Doctor Profiles & Specialties

│   └── Quality Indicators
├── Support & Resources
│   ├── Frequently Asked Questions

### 1.2 Content Classification System

**Primary Content Categories:**

1. **Informational Content**
   - Program explanations
   - Benefit descriptions
   - Process guides
   - Educational materials

2. **Interactive Content**
   - Enrollment forms
   - Health assessments
   - Doctor search interfaces
   - Appointment booking systems

3. **Multimedia Content**
   - Explainer videos (4 languages)
   - Interactive infographics

   - Audio guides

4. **Dynamic Content**
   - Real-time clinic availability
   - Live appointment scheduling

**3.1.3 Translation Workflow**

```
Content Creation → Translation → Review → Cultural Adaptation → QA → Publication
      ↓              ↓         ↓              ↓              ↓          ↓
   English      Mandarin   Cultural     Technical     Validation   Release
   Master      Translation  Review      Adaptation    Testing     All Languages

## 8. Risk Management & Mitigation

### 8.1 Content-Related Risks

**8.1.1 Medical Accuracy Risks**
- **Risk:** Outdated or incorrect medical information
- **Impact:** High - potential health consequences
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Regular expert review cycles
  - Automated medical terminology checking
  - Source validation requirements
  - Rapid update processes

**8.1.2 Cultural Sensitivity Risks**
- **Risk:** Culturally inappropriate content
- **Impact:** High - user alienation and trust loss
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Cultural advisor review process
  - Community feedback integration
  - Cultural sensitivity testing
  - Diverse review panel composition

**8.1.3 Translation Quality Risks**
- **Risk:** Poor translation quality affecting comprehension
- **Impact:** Medium - user experience degradation
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Professional translation requirements
  - Native speaker review process
  - User testing with native speakers
  - Quality assurance scoring system

### 8.2 Technical Implementation Risks

**8.2.1 Performance Risks**
- **Risk:** Slow loading multilingual content
- **Impact:** Medium - user abandonment
- **Probability:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Content caching strategies
  - Progressive loading implementation
  - CDN optimization
  - Performance monitoring

**8.2.2 Accessibility Risks**
- **Risk:** Non-compliant accessibility features
- **Impact:** High - legal and ethical concerns
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - WCAG 2.1 AA compliance auditing
  - Assistive technology testing
  - User testing with disabilities
  - Continuous accessibility monitoring

### 8.3 Content Governance Risks

**8.3.1 Quality Control Risks**
- **Risk:** Inadequate quality control processes
- **Impact:** High - credibility and effectiveness loss
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Comprehensive QA framework
  - Multiple review layers
  - Automated quality checking
  - Regular process audits

**8.3.2 Update Management Risks**
- **Risk:** Outdated content without timely updates
- **Impact:** Medium - program effectiveness
- **Probability:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Automated update scheduling
  - Trigger-based update processes
  - Content lifecycle management
  - Regular content audits
