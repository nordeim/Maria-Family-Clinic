## 1. Program Overview

### 1.1 What is Healthier SG?

Healthier SG is a national initiative by Singapore's Ministry of Health (MOH) launched in July 2023, focusing on preventive healthcare and primary care reform. The program aims to help all Singaporeans take proactive steps to manage their health and quality of life through personalized health plans with family doctors.

**Key Objectives:**
- Preventive healthcare approach
- One resident, one family doctor
- Personalized health plans
- National enrollment program
- Subsidized screenings and vaccinations
- Chronic disease management

### 1.2 Program Vision

The program envisions transforming Singapore's healthcare system from reactive treatment to proactive prevention, ensuring every resident has a trusted family doctor who can provide holistic health management.

## 2. Eligibility Criteria

### 2.1 Primary Eligibility Requirements

**Age Requirement:**
- All Singapore Citizens (SC) and Permanent Residents (PR)
- Minimum age: 40 years old
- No upper age limit

**Residency Status:**
- Singapore Citizens
- Singapore Permanent Residents
- Must be physically present in Singapore

### 2.2 Priority Enrollment Groups

**Phase 1 (Started July 2023):**
- Singapore residents aged 60 years and above
- Chronic disease patients (diabetes, hypertension, high cholesterol)
- High-risk individuals

**Phase 2 (2024-2025):**
- Ages 40-59
- All eligible residents regardless of health status

**Current Status (2025):**
- All eligible residents aged 40+ can enroll
- Continuous enrollment open

## 4. Enrollment Process & Requirements

### 4.1 Self-Enrollment Process

**Step 1: SMS Invitation**
- Receive SMS invite from MOH
- Eligible residents automatically notified
- Can also self-register without invitation

**Step 2: HealthHub Registration**
- Download HealthHub mobile app
- Create account with SingPass
- Access enrollment portal

**Step 3: Choose Clinic & Doctor**
- Select participating Healthier SG clinic
- Choose family doctor (must be enrolled doctor)
- Confirm enrollment details

**Step 4: Health Plan Consultation**
- Schedule first consultation within 3 months
- Doctor develops personalized health plan
- Receive $20 Healthpoints bonus

### 4.2 Caregiver-Assisted Enrollment

**Process:**
- Grant caregiver access through HealthHub
- Caregiver helps with enrollment process
- Assistance for elderly or technologically challenged residents
- Maintains patient's autonomy and consent

### 4.3 Clinic Selection Criteria

**Important Considerations:**
- Location convenience
- Doctor-patient compatibility
- Clinic operating hours
- Available services
- Language capabilities
- Insurance acceptance (MediSave, CHAS)

## 11. Myths vs. Facts

### 11.1 Common Misconceptions Debunked

**Myth 1:** "Healthier SG will increase medical expenses"
**Fact:** Program provides subsidies and reduces out-of-pocket costs for screenings, vaccinations, and consultations.

**Myth 2:** "Only sick people need to enroll"
**Fact:** Program focuses on prevention for all residents aged 40+, regardless of current health status.

**Myth 3:** "Enrollment means losing doctor choice"
**Fact:** Residents can choose their preferred participating doctor and clinic.

**Myth 4:** "It's difficult to change doctors"
**Fact:** MOH allows changes with proper notification and documentation.

**Myth 5:** "Only one clinic type is allowed"
**Fact:** Both private GP clinics and polyclinics participate in the program.

### 11.2 Program Facts Summary

- Voluntary enrollment program
- No mandatory health assessments before enrollment
- Free to join and participate
- Supports preventive healthcare approach
- Enhances doctor-patient relationships
- Improves healthcare accessibility
- Reduces financial barriers to preventive care

## 12. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 12.1 General Questions

**Q: Who can enroll in Healthier SG?**
A: All Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents aged 40 years and above are eligible to enroll.

**Q: Is enrollment mandatory?**
A: No, enrollment is completely voluntary and residents can choose to participate or not.

**Q: Can I enroll if I don't have any health issues?**
A: Yes, the program focuses on preventive healthcare for all eligible residents.

**Q: How long does the enrollment process take?**
A: The enrollment itself takes just a few minutes, but you must schedule your first consultation within 3 months.

### 12.2 Benefits & Coverage Questions

**Q: What subsidies do I receive?**
A: Eligible participants receive subsidies for health screenings, vaccinations, and chronic disease management under the Healthier SG Chronic Tier.

**Q: Is the first consultation free?**
A: While not free, you receive $20 worth of Healthpoints after your first Health Plan consultation.

**Q: Are all screenings covered?**
A: Nationally recommended screenings are fully subsidized for eligible Singapore Citizens.

**Q: Can I still use my existing insurance?**
A: Yes, you can still use your existing insurance plans including MediSave and MediShield Life.

### 12.3 Doctor & Clinic Selection

**Q: Can I choose my doctor?**
A: Yes, you can select any participating doctor who is registered with Healthier SG.

**Q: What if I want to change my doctor later?**
A: You can request a change through MOH with proper documentation and reason.

**Q: Are all clinics participating?**
A: No, only clinics with proper accreditation (CHAS, CDMP, etc.) can participate.

**Q: Can I enroll at a polyclinic instead of a private clinic?**
A: Yes, both private GP clinics and polyclinics participate in the program.
